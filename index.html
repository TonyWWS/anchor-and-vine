Our Story

A love letter to the Whitsundays.

Anchor & Vine was born from a simple idea: this coastline deserved a restaurant as beautiful as the view. We source our seafood daily from local fishermen at the marina, pair it with North Queensland produce, and serve it with the warmth that only comes from genuinely loving where you live.

Our menu changes with the seasons and the catch. What doesn't change is the commitment to honest, unfussy cooking that lets the ingredients speak for themselves — and a wine list that makes the most of Queensland's small producers.
